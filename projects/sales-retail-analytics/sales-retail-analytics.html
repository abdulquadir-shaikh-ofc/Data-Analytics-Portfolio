Sales & Retail Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive multi-page interactive dashboard analyzing global retail performance, product trends, regional insights, and customer behavior patterns.

Published June 2026
Data Points ~60,000 transactions | $24.5M total sales
Report Pages 4 interactive pages
Technology Power BI Desktop, DAX

Project Documentation

Project Overview

This dashboard demonstrates expert-level Power BI proficiency through a multi-page, fully interactive analytical solution. The project showcases advanced data modeling, DAX optimization, and professional visual design across four complementary reporting perspectives.

Dataset & Preparation

Source: Kaggle Global Superstore Dataset (transformed and enhanced)

Scope:

  • ~60,000 transaction records
  • 5 product categories (original 3 + 2 synthetic: Electronics & Gadgets, Health & Wellness)
  • 23 sub-categories across global markets
  • Total sales: $24.5M with diverse geographic and temporal distribution

Data Transformations Applied:

  • Date Shifting: Dataset dates adjusted so the most recent order aligns with mid-2026 (current reporting period)
  • Product ID Reconstruction: Rebuilt product identifiers to enforce strict one-to-one mapping between product names and IDs, eliminating data quality anomalies
  • Lifecycle Segmentation: Added Product.Lifecycle column classifying products into New, Growing, Declining, or Mature using comparable year-over-year (YoY) window logic (Jan 1–Jun 18, both years) to avoid partial-year bias
  • Synthetic Category Generation: Created realistic, differentiated product categories (Electronics & Gadgets, Health & Wellness) with appropriate pricing and volume distributions to add portfolio diversity
  • Sales Restructuring: Redistributed sales volumes to reflect realistic market dynamics across all categories

Report Architecture (4 Pages)

Page 1: Executive Overview

  • High-level KPI summary (Total Sales, Profit, Margin %, Order Count)
  • Year-to-date performance vs. prior period trends
  • Sales by category overview with profit contribution breakdown
  • Top 10 products by revenue

Page 2: Product Analysis

  • Product lifecycle segmentation (New, Growing, Declining, Mature)
  • Category performance heatmap with detailed metrics
  • Product performance ranking with margin analysis
  • Sales trend analysis by product lifecycle stage
  • Profitability vs. volume scatter analysis

Page 3: Regional Performance

  • Geographic heat map showing regional sales density
  • Top performing countries and regions
  • Regional margin analysis and profitability comparison
  • Market penetration and growth trends by region

Page 4: Customer Analytics

  • RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) segmentation
  • Customer lifetime value distribution
  • Repeat purchase analysis and customer retention
  • Customer acquisition and churn trends

Key Metrics & Advanced DAX Measures

The dashboard includes 100+ optimized DAX measures covering:

  • Revenue Analysis: Total Sales, Average Order Value, Sales Growth YoY, Sales per Customer
  • Profitability: Gross Profit, Profit Margin %, Operating Margin, Margin by Category
  • Customer Metrics: Total Customers, New Customers, Repeat Customers, Customer Retention Rate, Customer Lifetime Value
  • Product Performance: Product Count, Average Price, Units Sold, Product Turnover
  • Time-based Analytics: Period comparisons, rolling calculations, YoY growth rates
  • Advanced Calculations: Lifecycle-based aggregations, regional benchmarking, customer segmentation

Sample DAX patterns include: CALCULATE with FILTER, SUMX/SUMPRODUCT for weighted calculations, RANKX for ranking, date-based DATESBETWEEN for dynamic period selection, and IF/SWITCH for conditional logic.

Design & Interactivity

Visual Design: Custom Power BI theme with consistent color palette, typography, and spacing ensuring a professional, polished appearance across all pages.

Interactive Elements:

  • Slicers for date range, product category, region, and customer segment filtering
  • Cross-filtering between visualizations for cohesive exploration
  • Drill-through capabilities from summary to detail levels
  • Bookmark-driven navigation for user-guided exploration paths

Technical Implementation

Development Environment: Power BI Desktop (offline-first approach)

Publishing: Power BI "Publish to Web" feature for public, non-authenticated access

Data Model: Optimized relational schema with proper cardinality relationships (1:* from Dimension to Fact tables), avoiding bridge table anti-patterns and ensuring efficient DAX evaluation.

Performance Considerations:

  • Aggregated fact tables for executive-level summaries
  • Calculated columns used sparingly; measures preferred for flexibility
  • Query folding leveraged where applicable to push computation to source
