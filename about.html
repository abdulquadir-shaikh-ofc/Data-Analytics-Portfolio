About Me

I'm Abdulquadir, an expert-level Power BI analyst and data visualization specialist passionate about transforming complex business data into actionable insights through interactive, professional dashboards.

Expertise

With deep expertise in Power BI and DAX, I specialize in designing and building production-grade analytical solutions that enable data-driven decision making across organizations. My work spans:

  • Power BI Development: Desktop report design, advanced visualizations, interactive filtering, drill-through capabilities, and performance optimization
  • DAX Mastery: Complex measure authoring, time-based calculations, contextual aggregations, and optimization for enterprise-scale models
  • Data Modeling: Designing efficient relational schemas, enforcing proper cardinality, and optimizing for analytical performance
  • Data Transformation: ETL pipeline design, data quality assurance, and Python-based data preparation
  • Business Analytics: KPI identification, metric definition, insight synthesis, and actionable dashboard design

Portfolio Philosophy

Rather than relying on generic Kaggle datasets, I build differentiated portfolio projects that demonstrate:

  • Rigorous data preparation and quality assurance practices
  • Sophisticated analytical techniques (lifecycle segmentation, RFM analysis, time-series forecasting)
  • Professional multi-page report architecture with intuitive user navigation
  • Custom synthetic data generation showing domain understanding and realism
  • Production-ready code and design patterns suitable for enterprise deployment
